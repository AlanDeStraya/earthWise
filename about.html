About

Hi, my name is Riana and I am deeply passionate about living sustainably. There is no denying the huge environmental toll that humans are having on the planet, and I believe we all can – and in fact, must – do our part to live in a way that does not compromise the future of the Earth or its many inhabitants.

I decided to create EarthWise after spending several years working to reduce my own environmental impact. I realized that, although there are many other wonderful resources that offer great information about living more eco-consciously, it can be hard to find and follow their advice. I wanted to consolidate as many tips and guidelines in one place as possible, so that anyone can easily incorporate them into their own life.

I hope this website helps you live more simply and more sustainably. If you have any questions, comments or ideas to share, please get in touch!
